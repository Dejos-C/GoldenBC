Welcome to the Golden Friendly Basketbal League website! Located in the beautiful town of Golden, British Columbia, our league is dedicated to providing a safe and welcoming environment for basketball players of all skill levels and backgrounds. Our community is diverse, with members hailing from different races, ethnicities, and cultures. However, we believe that basketball has the power to bring people together and build lasting friendships. Through our league, we showcase the talents of our players while promoting inclusivity, respect, and sportsmanship. Whether you're a seasoned player or just looking to have some fun on the court, we invite you to join us and experience the joy of basketball in a supportive and friendly environment.
